# City of Exiles

![Utsa Paradisa, the City of Exiles](assets/home-hero.png){ .home-hero }

Welcome to the world of **Erratt** — a land still bleeding from **the Schism**,
the day the Feywild tried to fold itself into the material plane. Magic and Fey
spilled across the world, the ground itself was split, and the beasts of the
forests woke into thought. Every border, grudge, and alliance that follows was
drawn in that wound's shadow.

Our story begins at the edge of the map, in **Utsa Paradisa** — the Jewel of the
Desert, the **City of Exiles**. It is a walled oasis where water is sacred because
it is scarce, and where the cast-out of a hundred dead nations have come to build
something like a home. This is the session archive and campaign wiki: a record of
who the exiles are, what they've done, and the world that keeps testing them.

## The Schism

The Schism was no war and no plague — it was the moment two worlds nearly became
one. When the Feywild pressed against the material plane, it tore the land apart,
flooded Erratt with raw magic and Fey denizens, and awakened ordinary forest
animals into the sentient **Beastfolk**. The great powers that survived did so by
adapting: dwarves and orcs ended centuries of mountain war with the **Iron Grey
Concordat**, Drathomir carved new territory from the ashes of the **Deadwood**, and
the Eladrin — blamed for it all — have been mistrusted ever since. Nothing in
Erratt is untouched by it.

## The City of Exiles

[Utsa Paradisa](wiki/locations/utsa-paradisa.md) is home to those who could go
nowhere else: predominantly **Tieflings** shunned by nations that no longer exist,
a large **Tabaxi** population, and other exiled beastfolk. Here, control of water
is control of everything — it is guarded, rationed, and revered, its rites tended
at [the Water Temple](wiki/locations/the-water-temple.md) and its peace kept by the
[City Guard](wiki/factions/city-guard-of-utsa-paradisa.md). From the crowded
[Bazaar](wiki/locations/the-bazaar.md) and the tortoise-shell homes of
[the Slums](wiki/locations/the-slums.md) to the wealth of
[the Jade District](wiki/locations/the-jade-district.md) and the halls of
[the University](wiki/locations/the-university.md), it is a city of outcasts
carving out belonging amid scarcity and prejudice.

Utsa Paradisa punches far above its size. **Empress Selene** brokered the tenuous
peace between Drathomir and the Goliaths, giving the city real diplomatic weight,
and its lifeline runs north to **UkRuundir**, the Goliath capital — trading tar and
spices for lumber and mammoth meat across a brutal desert-to-cold-forest crossing
that few dare to make.

## The Wider World

Beyond the desert lie the three great nations: cosmopolitan **Trivan** at the
bottom of the Schism, the dwarven-and-orc **Iron Holds**, the dragonborn realm of
**Drathomir**, and **Medui-Ghislerin**, the elven Tree of Five Families. Eastward
stretch the **Riverlands** and the **Emerald Wilderness**, tended by halflings and
firbolgs; northward, past Utsa Paradisa, sprawl the untamed **Northern
Territories**. It is a crowded, quarrelsome world of old grudges and older gods —
and the exiles are only just stepping into it.
