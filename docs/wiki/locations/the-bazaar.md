# The Bazaar

The marketplace of [Utsa Paradisa](utsa-paradisa.md), where a bazaar ledger-man
keeps the accounts. In an earlier session the party looted the Bazaar amid the
chaos — and [Behnsun Payne](../pcs/behnsun-payne.md) so frightened the ledger-man
that he ate the ledger recording the stolen goods.

## Session History

- **[The Tidewater Amulet](../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Left ransacked but no
  longer under immediate threat, held by [Major Jamieson](../npcs/major-jamieson.md)
  and the remaining guards while the party moved on to other districts.
