# The University

A district of [Utsa Paradisa](utsa-paradisa.md) home to the city's wizards and
their tower. During the citywide assault it was the district that fared best,
held by "a lot of strong wizards."

## Who You'll Find Here

[Krum](../pcs/krum.md)'s uncle lives here, and [Adarius](../pcs/adarius.md) is
said to have connections among its scholars.

## Session History

- **[The Tidewater Amulet](../../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Reported intact by
  Seris's aerial scouting; shouts of victory rose from the Wizard Tower as the
  city was secured for the night.
