# The Water Temple

The temple in [the Jade District](the-jade-district.md) of
[Utsa Paradisa](utsa-paradisa.md) where the water priestess
[Naivara Tidewoven](../npcs/naivara-tidewoven.md) performs the rites that cleanse
the city's water and keep it flowing. In a city where water is sacred and scarce,
it is among the most vital sites in Utsa Paradisa.

## Session History

- **[The Tidewoven Amulet](../../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — The session's climactic
  battle. The party arrived — famously, by catapulting one another across the
  battlefield — to find suture flies stitching Naivara's mouth and hands shut. The
  guard commander [Dalen Marr](../npcs/dalen-marr.md) held the line, but the
  flies carried the priestess out of the city. Before she vanished she dropped a
  teardrop amulet to the party — the session's central clue.
