# Charles Dammit

**Role:** Guardsman of the [City Guard of Utsa Paradisa](../factions/city-guard-of-utsa-paradisa.md)

## Overview

A city guard the party named in an earlier session, since revealed to be a
coward. When the city came under attack he fled and hid rather than hold his
post. [Major Jamieson](major-jamieson.md) intends to make him an "office boy" as
punishment, and [Krum](../pcs/krum.md) is no friend of his.
