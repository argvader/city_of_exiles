# Dalen Marr

**Role:** Veteran commander of the [City Guard of Utsa Paradisa](../factions/city-guard-of-utsa-paradisa.md)

## Overview

A rugged, older guard commander — one of the elder officers, and the man
[Krum](../pcs/krum.md) reports to directly. He fights with grim humor, standing
his ground at [the Water Temple](../locations/the-water-temple.md) and roaring at
the attackers even when blinded by smoke: "I still see you, you pieces of shit."

## Session History

- **[The Tidewater Amulet](../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Held the line at the
  Water Temple through the final battle, taking hits meant for the party and
  surviving the assault that carried off
  [Nybora Tidewater](nybora-tidewater.md).
