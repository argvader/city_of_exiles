# Frederick

**Role:** Talking fey amulet carried by [Fizz](../pcs/fizz.md)

## Overview

Frederick is a talking wooden amulet — a fey being bound into an idol — that
[Fizz](../pcs/fizz.md) carries and converses with. Only Fizz can hear him, which
makes for some one-sided conversations with a piece of wood. Frederick is
crotchety and comic, quick to withhold his trust, and speaks fondly (and
protectively) of someone he calls "mom."

## Relationships

- Recognized the spirit in [Naivara Tidewoven](naivara-tidewoven.md)'s teardrop —
  [the Amulet Speaker](the-amulet-speaker.md) — as a "brother," and confirmed
  that Fizz, like [Prance](../pcs/prance-galavant.md), has been "chosen" by a fey
  vessel.

## Session History

- **[The Tidewoven Amulet](../../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Vouched (grudgingly) for
  the new amulet and its quest, tying Fizz into the same fey mystery now driving
  Prance.
