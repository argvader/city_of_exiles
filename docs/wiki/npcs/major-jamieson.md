# Major Jamieson

**Role:** Officer of the [City Guard of Utsa Paradisa](../factions/city-guard-of-utsa-paradisa.md)

## Overview

A city guardsman who fell victim to the suture flies during the assault on
[Utsa Paradisa](../locations/utsa-paradisa.md): his hands were sewn to his thighs
and his lips stitched shut. The party found him panicking and freed him with a
series of medicine checks and a healing touch.

## Personality

Proud and duty-bound. The moment his mouth was freed — spitting blood — his first
concern was rallying the remaining guards to hold [the Bazaar](../locations/the-bazaar.md).
He has no patience for cowards: he wants the deserter
[Charles Dammit](charles-dammit.md) put to work as his "office boy" once the
crisis passes.

## Session History

- **[The Tidewoven Amulet](../../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Freed by
  [Fizz](../pcs/fizz.md), [Behnsun Payne](../pcs/behnsun-payne.md), and
  [Prance Galavant](../pcs/prance-galavant.md), who unstitched his mouth and hand
  and healed his wounds while [Krum](../pcs/krum.md) kept him calm.
