# Naivara Tidewoven

**Role:** Water Genasi priestess of [Utsa Paradisa](../locations/utsa-paradisa.md)
· **Status:** Abducted

## Overview

A water Genasi priestess and one of the most important figures in the City of
Exiles — by the DM's own reckoning, "besides the Empress, she may be the most
important person in the city." Naivara cleanses the water when it is corrupted and
prays to the goddess of water to keep it flowing into Utsa Paradisa, a city where
[water is sacred and scarce](../locations/utsa-paradisa.md).

## Appearance

A fish-like water Genasi. When the party found her at
[the Water Temple](../locations/the-water-temple.md), her fingers were already
burning from the assault.

## Session History

- **[The Tidewoven Amulet](../../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Ambushed at the Water
  Temple by suture flies, which sewed her mouth and then her hands shut to stop
  her praying, and carried her bodily out of the city. As she was flown away, she
  tore a gold teardrop amulet from her chest and dropped it — deliberately —
  onto [Prance Galavant](../pcs/prance-galavant.md)'s ear. The amulet holds a
  trapped fey being who calls her "my daughter" and begs the party to save her.
