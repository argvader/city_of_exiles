# The Dragonborn Band

**Role:** Troupe of dragonborn musicians · **Affiliation:** wandering
performers of [Utsa Paradisa](../locations/utsa-paradisa.md)

## Overview

A troupe of dragonborn troubadours the party first crossed at a chariot race — a
red dragonborn singer with an Earth-Wind-and-Fire soul to his voice, a big
dragonborn drummer, and a blue dragonborn among them. Old rivals turned allies,
they remember the party as "the fuckers that fixed dark hearts" and admit the
chariot race "was well played."

## Abilities

When they play, their music grants inspiration (a d6 to add to a roll) to nearby
fighters — and, pooling their magic once a day, they can lay hands and heal the
wounded. Members trade element-nicknames: **Earth**, a hulking brown-scaled
healer; **Fire**; and **Water**, who took a shine to
[Prance Galavant](../pcs/prance-galavant.md).
