# Adarius

**Player:** Josh · **Ancestry:** Human · **Class:** Wizard

The party's arcane mind — sharp, skeptical, and rarely without a dry remark.

## Overview

Adarius is the group's resident spellcaster and its loudest voice of caution.
When the party recovered a mysterious talking amulet, it was Adarius who
ritually cast *identify* on it — and Adarius who immediately wanted to sell the
"talking asshole necklace" rather than answer its call.

## Ties to the City

His parents live in [the Slums](../locations/the-slums.md) of
[Utsa Paradisa](../locations/utsa-paradisa.md) — a hardy father the party came to
know as "Bob" and a nimble mother. During the defense of the Slums, his father
was caught in a magma creature's fire and badly burned, but survived thanks to
the party and the healing magic of [the Dragonborn Band](../npcs/the-dragonborn-band.md).
Adarius is also said to have connections at [the University](../locations/the-university.md).
