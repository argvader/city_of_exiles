# Behnsun Payne

**Player:** Jonathan · **Ancestry:** Human · **Class:** Sorcerer

"It's Behnsun Payne — and healing is my game." A gruff sorcerer with a soft spot
he'd rather not admit to.

## Overview

Behnsun leans on *chill touch*, *chromatic orb*, and *firebolt*, and keeps a
rapier for flourish. His spell list ran thin against the fire creatures plaguing
the city — most of them shrugged off flame — which forced him to get creative
with cold and necrotic damage.

## Ties to the City

Behnsun's roots are in [the Slums](../locations/the-slums.md). He looks after an
orphanage there — two children, **Cadet Fox** and **Cadet Tiger**, call him
"dad" — and has history with an old crew who run a tavern in the district. Last
session he frightened a bazaar ledger-man badly enough that the man ate the
ledger recording the party's stolen goods.

## Session History

- **[The Tidewater Amulet](../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Helped free the
