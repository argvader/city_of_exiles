# Fizz

**Player:** Kevin · **Ancestry:** Myconid · **Class:** Bard

A friendly mushroom-man performer who measures success by how many friends he
can make.

## Overview

Fizz supports the party with bardic inspiration, *song of rest*, and a light
crossbow, and carries a respectable medicine kit. He has a fanbase among the
common folk of [the Slums](../locations/the-slums.md) — "a lot of my customers,"
as he put it when the party chose where to make their stand.

## Frederick

Fizz carries **[Frederick](../npcs/frederick.md)**, a talking wooden amulet that
houses a fey being. Over the course of the session it was revealed that Frederick
has "chosen" Fizz — marking him, like [Prance](prance-galavant.md), as bound to a
fey vessel and its purposes.

## Session History

- **[The Tidewoven Amulet](../../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Freed
  [Major Jamieson](../npcs/major-jamieson.md)'s sewn-shut mouth with a delicate
  medicine check, felled a suture fly with a clean crossbow shot, and used
  Frederick to confirm that both he and Prance had been "chosen" by kindred fey
  spirits.
