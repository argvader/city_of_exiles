# Krum

**Player:** Matt · **Ancestry:** Dwarf · **Class:** Rogue

A hardy dwarf rogue — low on hit points, high on nerve.

## Overview

Krum fights with paired daggers and a crossbow, leaning on sneak attack and a
rogue's cunning action to dart around the battlefield. He spent the session
convinced he'd die in every encounter ("I made my peace with this") and came out
the other side as its biggest hero.

## Ties to the City

Krum has an uncle at [the University](../locations/the-university.md), and is
known to the guard — he reports directly to the veteran commander
[Dalen Marr](../npcs/dalen-marr.md). He is not on friendly terms with the coward
[Charles Dammit](../npcs/charles-dammit.md).

## Session History

- **[The Tidewoven Amulet](../../sessions/2026-07-03.md)** — Twice let the party
  build a makeshift catapult and launch him across the battlefield to crash down
  on fire mephits, driving them so far into the earth that their death-bursts
  barely singed him. Also calmed the panicking Major Jamieson long enough for the
  party to unstitch him.
