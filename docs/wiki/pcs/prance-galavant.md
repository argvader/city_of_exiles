# Prance Galavant

**Player:** Gary · **Ancestry:** Harengon · **Class:** Paladin

A theatrical rabbit-knight who narrates his own legend as he lives it — "the
greatest of heroes is here to improve the situation greatly."

## Overview

Prance is a Harengon paladin who fights with a two-handed longsword and split
mail, heals allies with *lay on hands*, and leans on his rabbit traits — lucky
footwork on failed saves and a knack for landing brutal critical hits. He speaks
in grand, ceremonial flourishes and treats every fight as the next act of an
unfolding story. Where others loot, Prance postures.

## The Tidewoven Amulet

When [Naivara Tidewoven](../npcs/naivara-tidewoven.md) was carried off, she draped a
teardrop amulet over Prance's ear as a deliberate clue. The fey being inside
chose him — "You will do. You must save my daughter." Against
[Adarius](adarius.md)'s strong objections, Prance donned the amulet and swore an
oath to rescue the priestess, feeling "a spark of magic click" as he put it on.
